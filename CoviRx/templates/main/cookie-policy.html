Definition and uses of cookies in this site

A cookie is a small text file that’s stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website.

What kind of cookies does this website use?

Session cookies: These are stored while you’re browsing. They get deleted from your device when you close your browser e.g. Google Chrome or Safari. Cookies include sessionid, csrftoken.

Third party cookies (Google Charts, Google Auth, Google reCAPTCHA): These cookies are set when using the various external components in the website. Google Charts helps developers to create charts and it has been used in Home and Admin pages, Google reCAPTCHA has been used in Contact and Drug Contribution pages, Google Auth has been used for login.

Rejection or elimination of cookies

You can allow, block or delete the cookies installed on your computer through your browser’s settings options, in which case you may not be able to access certain areas of the website. If you wish to know how to manage your browser’s cookies, please access the following links:
